Common Mistakes That Kill Real Estate Campaigns

🚫 Targeting Mistakes — Avoid These

Mistake #1: Using "Everyone in this location" instead of "People LIVING in"

Impact: Tourists, delivery workers, and people just passing through will see your ads. They'll never buy a plot in Kanchipuram.

Fix: ALWAYS select "People living in this location" for every adset.

Mistake #2: Keeping Advantage+ Audience ON for a new campaign

Impact: Meta will ignore your interests and show ads to random people. Good for established accounts with lots of conversion data, but terrible for new campaigns.

Fix: Switch OFF for Weeks 1-4. Only test Advantage+ in Month 2 after you have 50+ lead conversions.

Mistake #3: Adding interests to a Lookalike audience

Impact: Shrinks audience from 2M to 200K. Meta can't optimize delivery with such a small pool. High CPM, high CPL.

Fix: Lookalike = ONLY location + age. No interests, no behaviors, no narrowing.

Mistake #4: Using "Lowest Cost" bid strategy

Impact: Meta will spend your ENTIRE budget with no CPL control. Could get leads at ₹50 or ₹5,000 — no predictability.

Fix: Always use "Cost Cap" at ₹350 for this campaign. Meta will aim to deliver leads near this price.

Mistake #5: Not excluding existing leads

Impact: Same person fills your form 3 times. You think you have 30 leads but actually have 20 unique people. CPL is actually 50% higher than dashboard shows.

Fix: Create Custom Audience from Lead Form submissions, exclude it from ALL adsets.

Mistake #6: Audience overlap between adsets

Impact: Your adsets compete against each other in the auction. You bid against yourself and pay higher CPM.

Fix: Check overlap: Audiences → Select 2 audiences → "Show Audience Overlap." If > 30% overlap, exclude one adset's audience from the other.

Mistake #7: Audience too small (under 200K)

Impact: Same people see your ad 5-10 times in a week. Ad fatigue = CTR drops = CPL skyrockets.

Fix: Target audience must be at least 500K for ₹1,000+/day budget. If too small, remove some "Narrow Audience" restrictions or expand location radius.
